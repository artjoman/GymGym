# Google Play — Store listing copy

Ready-to-paste text for the Play Console listing. Character caps noted; verified
against Google's limits.

> (`app/src/main/res/values/strings.xml` → `app_name` = "GymGym"), which stays
> short so it isn't truncated under the icon. Don't rename `app_name`.

## Full description  (max 4000)

```
Stop counting in your head. GymGym turns your phone camera into an AI rep
counter — prop it up, pick an exercise, and it watches your form and counts
every rep, hands-free.

Powered by on-device AI pose detection, GymGym tracks your body in real time and
counts reps for squats, push-ups, pull-ups, and dumbbell press. No wearables, no
manual tapping.

WHAT YOU GET
• AI rep counter — automatic counting from your camera, with a spoken 3-2-1
  countdown and live rep call-outs.
• Auto-detect — not sure what to select? GymGym recognizes the exercise you're
  doing and locks it in.
• Hands-free voice control — say "next", "pause", "resume", or "reset"
  mid-workout without touching the phone.
• Workout plans — build multi-exercise routines with target reps and sets, then
  run them back-to-back while GymGym advances you automatically.
• History & stats — every set is saved. Track total reps, personal bests,
  average reps, time trained, and trends per exercise.
• Record & share — capture a set on video and send it to your trainer for form
  feedback in one tap.
• Form-first feedback — clear on-screen tracking cues so you know when you're in
  frame and counting.
• Backup & transfer — move your history, plans, and settings to a new device.

PRIVACY
Your camera is used only for on-device pose detection — frames are analyzed in
memory and never uploaded. Workouts, stats, and settings stay on your device.

EDITIONS
GymGym is free with occasional ads shown only between workouts — never during a
set. Prefer zero ads and fully offline? Grab the ad-free edition.

Keywords: rep counter, AI workout, camera rep tracker, gym trainer, form
tracker, squat counter, push-up counter, pull-up counter, hands-free workout,
workout tracker.

Created with love by projectorum.com
```

## ASO notes
- Title leads with **"Rep Counter"** — a lower-competition, high-intent query —
  plus **AI** for the camera differentiator.
- Keywords are woven into the short + full description naturally (Google
  penalizes keyword stuffing; there's no separate keyword field on Play).
- Consider localizing the title/description for your top markets later.
- Screenshots + a short promo video drive installs more than copy — use the
  arcade-style mascot (Reppo) demo and real counting footage.
